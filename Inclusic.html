Bringing your personal music taste to your personal digital library

Our Aim

People always want to listen to music. But, we don’t always have Wi-Fi. Sometimes you’re in the middle of the woods and just want to listen to some smooth jazz: or driving on a trip out of country and you need some ride tunes. No matter the case, Inclusic’s got you. Using any app you like, you can download your favourite songs into our app and listen anytime, anywhere. You can also sort your songs into playlists, use shuffle play features, and more!
